Frequently asked questions

Before your appointment.

What areas do you serve?

Patriot Detailing is designed to serve customers in and around Schwandorf and surrounding parts of Bavaria. Replace this text with your final service radius.

Do I need to provide water or electricity?

Appointment requirements depend on the selected service and equipment setup. We confirm access needs when approving your quote.

How long does a detail take?

A maintenance service may take a few hours, while heavily soiled interiors can require longer. Your estimate will be included with the quote.

Can you remove every stain or odor?

We use professional methods, but some permanent damage, dye transfer, or deeply embedded odors may not be completely removable. We set realistic expectations before beginning.

Do you wash vehicle exteriors at the customer’s home?

Only where local rules, drainage conditions, and wastewater controls allow it. Otherwise, exterior work must be completed at an approved location.
